## BIO

Klaus Dietrich, 25, is a Cincinnati-based car appraiser with a lifelong passion for classic and vintage automobiles. Holding a B.S. in Automotive Technology, Klaus combines formal education with hands-on knowledge to evaluate vehicles with precision and care. Known among friends and clients as a true car enthusiast, he spends his free time attending local car shows, exploring restoration techniques, and staying connected to the automotive community. Whether appraising a rare collectible or chatting about the finer details of design and history, Klaus brings both expertise and genuine excitement to the world of cars. He wants an application to help him keep track of his vintage cars' information and be notified when his cars needs maintenance. He also wants to select trusted gas stations to make sure he does not damage his vintage cars.

## GOALS

Identify what the user wants to be enabled to do

- I want to have dashboard where I can record maintaince history and track milage
- I want to be able to keep track of information about my car
- I want to be shown gas stations and select the ones I trust
- I want to be informed when my car needs maintenance

## PAIN POINTS

Identify pain points the user has with the current process or feature wishes missing from available solutions

- I struggle to keep track of past services (oil changes, tire rotations, repairs) across paper receipts, emails, and memory.
- I am unsure when upcoming services are due and often forget long-term items like brake pads or fluid flushes.
- Current apps and resources do not support my vintage cars.
- I have trouble locating trustworthy gas stations in one central place.
